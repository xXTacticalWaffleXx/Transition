A fight to the death against soul crushing bureaucracy

< >
In this photo I critique the fact that getting HRT is treated like a benign bureaucratic task just like paying taxes or getting an MOT for your car despite the fact that HRT is life saving medical treatement. Waiting for HRT even if you are lucky enough to be able to go private is still a war of attrition, where you have to survive living in your own body long enough for the gears of bureaucracy to turn and give you medication which could be months if you're lucky or if you are stuck with the NHS more likely years.
